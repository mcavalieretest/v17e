Insight of the Week

It's not every day that a business summit becomes a trending topic on Twitter. But last week's IBM Smarter Commerce Global Summit in Nashville did exactly that.

Becasue the event was filled with announcements, insightful sessions, smart speakers and held at a great venue, Tweets about the simmmit spread through social channels like a mesmerizing video of a midwest marching band. When we took a look under the hood, we saw that the Smarter Commerce buzz iextended far beyond the conference itself. These real-time social stats and visualizations of the event were provided by social media command center, presented by IBM business partner MutualMind.

At a glance

We're only getting better at social listening, and we're starting to see just how much information social data can deliver—and this is just scratching the surface.

  • Top discussion topics: Social, Data, Mobile, Watson, Engaging, Customers
  • Top influencers: @BryanKramer, @TedRubin, @PamMktgNut, @TamiCann, @rwang0, @briansolis, @kimgarst
  • Large volume of mentions: 21595
  • Highly Engaged: 46% of posts were retweets (based on #smartercommerce posts from May 17 to May 23)

Watson tops the list

By far, Watson created the most excitement about what future smarter commerce holds. We decided to take a look at what people were saying about Watson at the event by doing an advanced search on variations of Watson mentions within all the smarter commerce related mentions. This query yielded about 1800 metnions—not too shabby. The actual query, if you want to test out a different topic, is: watson OR #watson OR #ibmwatson OR @ibmwatson.
