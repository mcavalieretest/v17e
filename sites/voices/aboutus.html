What is Voices?

IBM Voices allows you to discover the most valuable social content shared, created and discussed by IBMers. You can use the site to connect and participate with key thought-leaders and subject matter experts around the technology and business issues that are most important to you. Learn with IBM to create a smarter planet and better understand the information that is shaping our world.

The REA Score of each content item is represented by these horizontal bars. IBM measures the performance of its social content by the metrics of Reach, Engagement and Amplification.

Reach

The number of times an update, post, or tweet has been seen by the followers, subscribers, or fans of its creator.

Engagement

The intentional user interactions with an update, post, or tweet.

Amplification

The spread of an update, post, or tweet due to re-sharing by followers, the friends of followers, and so on.
