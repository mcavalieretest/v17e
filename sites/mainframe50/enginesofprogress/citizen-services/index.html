Miami-Dade County, Zhuhai City and CNAV

Enabling improved services, faster insights, and continuous access to over 100 million citizens around the world

Government agencies in the U.S., China, and France are taking part in a new trend: empowering citizens through technology. These innovative, citizen-focused organizations are improving the lives of their constituents through increased transparency, robust services, and 24x7 access. And while they each differ in their approach to civic governance, they all have one thing in common: the IBM Mainframe is their core infrastructure platform. Watch the full story.

“Our goal is to provide better services for all of its residents to provide them more conveniently, to provide them at lower cost, and to expand the number of services that we do provide to make life much better.”

Adrienne Di Prima, Data Center Director, Miami-Dade County
