    Member Resources

    Ecosystem members are given access to a wealth of resources to help them create their applications built with Watson.

    Watson Developer Cloud

    As a Watson Developer, you’re given access to technology, tools and APIs, including training and ways to develop and test your applications built with Watson. Initially, the IBM team will be working with a limited number of partners, with plans of offering broader access to Ecosystem resources in the future. Learn how you can be one of the first to have access when technical content is made available.

    Watson Content Store

    Building an application that’s built with Watson requires the right kind of data and content. The Watson Content Store makes this possible by bringing together different sources of data for developers and content providers, including general knowledge, industry-specific content and subject matter expertise.

     

    Watson Talent Hub

    It's important to have access to the right people with the right skill sets to build a cognitive application. Whether it’s machine learning, user experience design or analytics, Ecosystem members can work with Watson experts on a fee or project basis.

    Direct investments

    The goal of the Watson Ecosystem is to nurture and support partners in developing commercial applications that incorporate Watson’s cognitive computing capabilities and collaborate on new commercial uses for Watson. To that end, IBM will invest $100M in select companies that develop a solution on Watson technology. As invested portfolio companies, partners can gain access to IBM’s technology, expertise, channels and client relationships.

    Criteria for equity investment include company size and growth rate, the ability of the application to positively disrupt and spur innovation in a market or industry, and level of committed resources.

    Want to get started?

    You can join the Ecosystem community as an Application Partner, a Content Partner or a Talent Partner.

    Application Partners

    The IBM Watson Ecosystem program is intended for companies interested in embedding Watson’s capability into their offerings to create a transformative cognitive app. For example, through the Watson Developer Cloud, a consumer-oriented application could deliver a personalized selling experience that replicates an in-store experience. Or, an airline could create a virtual travel agent app that recommends the perfect destination based on customer preferences.

    Content Partners

    The program is perfect for companies that have extensive amounts of data, but few good ways to share or monetize it. Likely candidates fitting this description are:

    • organizations that have the rights to use and/or license general and domain-specific content.
    • information-based organizations, like publishers, researchers, and even social forums can take advantage of the Watson Content Store.
    • organizations providing access to conventional data sources, representing billions of pages, including reports, publications, user-group logs, clinical trial data, call center recordings or product manuals, that could help fuel a new generation of cognitive apps.

    Talent Partners

    Want to be recognized as a Watson expert by your peers? We’re working on a multi-talent-level certification program, which we plan to offer later in 2014.

    Fluid, Inc.

    Fluid has developed the Fluid Expert Personal ShopperSM built with IBM Watson.

    The app calls upon Watson's ability to understand the nuances of human language and uncover answers from Big Data. Consumers who use Fluid's app will interact with rich media and dialogue with Watson, their newfound “cognitive, expert personal shopper.” The Fluid app incorporates the information users share and questions they ask to help them make smart, satisfying purchases by putting the most knowledgeable sales associate in the hands of consumers, every time.

    This provider of supply chain solutions for hospitals and healthcare systems has developed a powered-by-Watson application that allows clinical and financial users make timely, informed decisions to help improve quality, value, outcomes and patient satisfaction.     Welltok, Inc.

    Welltok is developing an app that will create Intelligent Health Itineraries™ for consumers.

    These personalized itineraries, sponsored by health plans, health systems and health retailers, will include tailored activities, relevant content and condition management programs, and reward users for engaging in healthy behaviors. Consumers who use Welltok's app -- CaféWell Concierge built with IBM Watson – will participate in conversations about their health with Watson. By using Watson's ability to learn from every interaction, the app will offer insights specific to individuals' health needs.
