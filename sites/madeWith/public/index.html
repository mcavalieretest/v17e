We live on a Smarter Planet, where data, mobile, social and cloud are transforming industries and allowing us to create value in new ways.

The evidence is abundant and everywhere.

See what the world's making today

92% of the people in the world own a mobile device. Businesses that are the best at consistently providing engaging mobile services can tap into new markets, customers and sources of revenue, making this technology the new imperative.

See how mobile is reaching more customers →

The IT infrastructure of the world is being transformed by the emergence of cloud computing—that is, the delivery of IT and business processes as digital services. Cloud enables new business models and the reinvention of core business practices.

See how businesses across the world are innovating with cloud →

Data is the world’s new natural resource and the basis of competitive advantage for businesses. Predictive and real-time analytics can help leaders in every industry create new value.

See how data is changing every industry →

Social has given rise to a new consumer with even greater expectations. Customers and employees expect to have social access to companies, and offering it can help improve both customer satisfaction and employee productivity.

IBM Watson solutions and technologies represent a first step into cognitive systems, a new era of computing, to enterprises, institutions and individuals via the cloud. Watson processes information by understanding natural language, generates hypotheses based on evidence, and, because it becomes more capable and precise, Watson will help leaders and organizations make better, more confident decisions.
